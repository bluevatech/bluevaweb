## Bad Queries

Poorly written queries can be responsible for a surprising amount of compute cost. Blueva Technologies tools and services can be utilized to help companies keep an eye out for when queries are running longer than they should, and can even kill those queries before undue expenses are incured.

## Unused Tables

As users use Data Assets, certain columns and even tables initially in scope will slowly fall out of favor and into disuse. As a result, IT teams are often paying compute and labor costs to load tables that are not being consumed by downstream end users. Our analytics package will provide usage stats on each table and column in your system, enabling you to make strategic decisions around managing your platform without your team having to spend valuable time tracking down unsused corners of your database.
